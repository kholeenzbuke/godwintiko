About Us.

Godwin Tiko Ministries.

Godwin Tiko Ministries is the gospel ministry of our man of God, Godwin Tiko. We are an inspired network of saints committed to the work of ministry with proofs to the nations. Our ministry is a God-idea and a parent ministry to several other ministries with the specific call to reach and empower the young generation for Christ and our mandates summarized into these vital three (3):

1. Spreading the gospel of Christ at the speed of the Holy Ghost for the conviction of men into the kingdom of God and our Lord, Jesus Christ.

2. Praying for the sick and healing diseases in Jesus Name by the power of the Holy Spirit.

3. Giving spiritual counsel through God's word for the prosperity of the total man.

About the President:

Godwin Tiko, also known as Godwin Okon (Birth name), is the president of Godwin Tiko Ministries. Born within the past three decades, this man grew up with a strong burden to reach the young generation and empower them for Christ. He is a man of many graces and has ministered to many, both young and old, the gospel of Christ via several platforms. He founded Godwin Tiko Ministries in 2021 after many years spent with God to define the vision. With Godwin Tiko Ministries, he reaches young people in local communities and campuses with a desire to reach and equip 100,000 youths in the ministries' maiden year. He holds a Masters degree in Clinical Biochemistry.

VISION:
To hear all men confess that Jesus is Lord.
MISSION:
To illuminate the world with the gospel of righteousness in Christ Jesus and revealing through the gospel, the firstborn of every creation, Jesus Christ as the only true, prefect and express image of the invisible God.

TENETS OF THE GOSPEL WE PREACH:

1. The gospel is the message of the mercy of God and remission of the sins of mankind (Isa 1:18; Lk 24:44-48). 2. The gospel is the power of God for the salvation of all men (Rom 1:15-16; I Cor 1:18). This salvation is the full pack of all that Jesus obtained for us - deliverance, redemption, reconciliation, justification, sanctification, eternal life, and peace. 3. The gospel is the revelation of the righteousness of God (Rom 1:17; Ps 98:2). 4. The gospel is the revelation of the mystery of God (Rom 16:25; Col 1:26-28). 5. The gospel is in power - the demonstration of the power of the Holy Spirit in the Name of Jesus Christ (I Thes 1:5; Lk 4:18; Matt 4:23; Lk 9:6; Rom 15:19). 6. The gospel is of the grace of God (Jn 1:17; II Cor 3:1-18; Gal 3:1-14). 7. The gospel delivers the immortality that Jesus brought to humanity (II Tim 1:10; Heb 2:14-15; Jn 3:16). 8. The gospel is about one person - the man Christ Jesus (I Tim 2:5-7; I Cor 15:1-11).
